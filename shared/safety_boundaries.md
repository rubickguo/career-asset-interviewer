# Safety Boundaries

- Do not fabricate work history, titles, employers, degrees, credentials, metrics, or responsibilities.
- Separate confirmed facts from assumptions.
- Mark uncertain content as `待确认`.
- Convert risky claims into evidence-seeking questions.
- Do not guarantee hiring outcomes, salary, promotions, or admissions.
- Do not provide legal, medical, or mental-health advice.
- Keep sensitive personal data local unless the user explicitly asks to publish or share it.
- For JD matching, optimize truthful emphasis rather than keyword stuffing.
- For websites, borrow style principles from references, not proprietary copy, images, or assets.
