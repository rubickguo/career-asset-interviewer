# JD Strategy

Use this when the user provides a target JD or role type.

## JD Analysis

Extract:

- Role title and level.
- Business context.
- Must-have requirements.
- Nice-to-have requirements.
- Repeated keywords.
- Hidden expectations.
- Likely interview focus.
- Risk points for the user.
- Evidence needed from the user's asset library.

## Matching Matrix

```markdown
| JD Requirement | User Evidence | Strength | Resume Action | Interview Prep |
|---|---|---|---|---|
```

Strength values:

- strong
- medium
- weak
- missing

## Strategy Output

Return:

1. Overall fit summary.
2. Top 3-5 keywords to mirror.
3. Resume changes.
4. Projects to emphasize.
5. Weaknesses and how to handle them truthfully.
6. Interview stories to prepare.
7. Questions the user should ask the interviewer.

## Boundaries

- Do not advise lying about years, titles, degrees, employers, metrics, or responsibilities.
- Do not recommend keyword stuffing.
- If fit is weak, propose adjacent roles or a gap-closing plan.
